Privacy Policy

Contact: stageauto.app@gmail.com

StageAuto respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the Software.

1. Information Collection

StageAuto does not collect or store personal data from your infrastructure files. Terraform and infrastructure files analyzed by StageAuto remain on your local machine.

2. License Validation

License validation requires an internet connection to verify license status. The validation request sends only license-related data (e.g., license key and device identifier) to the license server. No Terraform content is uploaded.

3. Payments

Payments and billing are processed by Paddle (Merchant of Record). StageAuto does not store payment card information.

4. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: stageauto.app@gmail.com
