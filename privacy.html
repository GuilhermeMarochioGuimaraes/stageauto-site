Privacy Policy

StageAuto respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the software.

Information Collection

StageAuto does not collect or store personal data from your infrastructure files.

License validation requires an internet connection to verify license status, but no sensitive infrastructure data is transmitted or stored.

Data Usage

Any files analyzed by StageAuto remain on your local machine. StageAuto does not upload or retain your Terraform or infrastructure files.

Third Parties

Payments and billing are processed securely by Paddle, our authorized payment provider. StageAuto does not store payment information.
